# HemaWeb

HemaWeb is a web-based platform that connects hospitals and medical centers with verified blood donors. It is designed to help reduce chronic blood shortages (initially focusing on Thailand) by making it easier to:

- Find eligible donors near active blood drives or urgent requests.
- Keep donors informed, engaged and reminded about when they can donate.
- Give hospitals a structured view of donors, verifications and donation history.

The system does **not** replace medical workflows or logistics; instead, it focuses on coordination, data, and communication between donors and partner medical organizations.

## Core idea

- **For donors** – a mobile-friendly web app where people can register, learn about blood donation, see nearby opportunities, track their eligibility and history, and receive targeted alerts when they are needed.
- **For medical centers** – a desktop web portal for staff, admins and super admins to verify donors, record donations, create blood drives and emergency requests, and manage accounts and activity logs.
- **For system administrators** – tools to onboard medical organizations, manage super admins and maintain informational content and global settings.
